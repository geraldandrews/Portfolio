About Me

Hello, my name is Gerald and I live in Bluefield, VA and I enjoy football, the outdoors and spending time with family. My interest for web development and graphic design began in 2013 when I started coding and styling components with HTML and CSS and editing photos. Since then I have been a self taught content creator and have devoted alot of my time into learning and improving my web development and graphic design skills through education, research, and designing and developing my own front-end web development and graphic design projects from scratch. In 2015, I graduated with an associates degree in Information Technology from Southwest Virginia Community College in Virginia. My passion and goal for each project I create is to learn something new, create something that is user friendly, mobile responsive, and stands out to people. My mission and goal is to eventually be able to use my experience, knowledge, and the skills I learn into creating useful content for companies or clients who could use my services.
